San Diego, California

Bordering Tijuana, Mexico, San Diego California is recognized by many for its near perfect weather, diverse shopping options and world famous theme parks. But San Diego is a captivating destination full of nuances that invite you to create your own version of the perfect vacation, from extreme sports to relaxing spas, a family day out or a night on the town, it's easy to find something for everyone. Enjoy a myriad of relaxing resorts, a diverse range of artistic and cultural events, haute cuisine and world-renowned attractions and 112 kilometers of spectacular coastline.

Downtown San Diego is a stunning urban center filled with stores, boutiques, hotels, restaurants, art galleries and theaters. The historic Gaslamp Quarter proudly displays its early 20th century elegance, while the modern Horton Plaza shopping center features more than 150 stores. The shopping visitor has plenty to choose from throughout the county, from state-of-the-art shopping malls, to elegant promenades with upscale boutiques, specialty stores, fine boutiques and galleries.
