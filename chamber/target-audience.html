Target

The target audience of this site is anyone that is involve or wants to be involve with entrepreneurs and the community.

Personas

Business Owner

This individual would be someone involve on business in any way, someone interest on getting involve with other entrepreneurs.

Community

This individual could be any member of the community, they might be interest on certain type of business or connection with the community.

Scenarios

  • A business owner that wants to create connections with the community.
  • A small business owner that wants to be known in the community.
  • A community member that is looking for certain type of business.
  • A person that wants to know about the community.
  • A company or person that is interest on certain products that the community sells.
